# manage anxiety
* take deep breaths. long slow exhale. remember you have value to bring.
* get present orientated. move around, listen to song, say tongue twister, talk to people, count backwards
# be mediocre
* puts less pressure on yourself. just get the communication done
# reframe circumstances
* use situation as opportunity to learn instead of hostile
# listening
* pace, space and grace
# frameworks
* what? so what? and now what?
* WHAT - W is what are we were, How are you connected to the event, A is anecdote about the event, T in thanks
# be concise (don't ramble)
* tell me the time, don't build the clock!
* know you audience, what is important
* goal: information, emotion and action
